## 1. Executive Summary

### 1.1 Product Overview
Dreamaker is a comprehensive life transition platform that helps users clarify their dreams, stay accountable, connect with others, and build a meaningful life. The MVP focuses on four core features that work together to provide clarity, support, and momentum during life transitions.

### 1.2 Problem Statement
People going through significant life transitions often feel overwhelmed, lose clarity about what they truly want, and lack the support systems needed to maintain momentum. Without structure and accountability, many abandon their authentic dreams and goals.

### 1.3 Solution
Dreamaker provides an integrated platform that combines vision clarity, community connection, reflection tools, and accountability tracking to help users navigate transitions while staying true to their authentic desires.

### 1.4 Success Metrics
- User engagement with all four core features
- Completion rate of life vision mapping
- Active participation in community features
- Progress tracking consistency
- User retention and satisfaction scores

## 2. User Personas

### 2.1 Primary Persona: The Transition Navigator
- **Demographics**: Ages 25-55, going through significant life change (career shift, relationship change, relocation, personal growth journey)
- **Pain Points**: 
  - Feeling overwhelmed by too many options and decisions
  - Losing sight of what they truly want
  - Lack of structure and accountability
  - Isolation during transition
- **Goals**: 
  - Gain clarity about their authentic desires
  - Build sustainable systems for progress
  - Connect with supportive community
  - Maintain momentum without burning out

### 2.2 Secondary Persona: The Dream Builder
- **Demographics**: Ages 20-65, actively pursuing multiple life goals
- **Pain Points**:
  - Difficulty seeing how different life areas connect
  - Lack of integrated view of their life vision
  - Need for better accountability systems
- **Goals**:
  - Create integrated life vision
  - Track progress across multiple areas
  - Maintain balance and integration

## 3. Core Features

### 3.1 Integrated Life Vision

#### 3.1.1 Feature Description
A comprehensive space where users can document their dreams and goals across different life areas (e.g., career, relationships, health, personal growth, finances, creativity, community, etc.). This feature helps users see the full picture of their life vision and understand how different areas integrate.

#### 3.1.2 User Stories
- As a user, I want to document my dreams for different life areas so I can see how they connect
- As a user, I want to visualize my integrated life vision so I understand what makes my life feel whole
- As a user, I want to update my vision as I grow so it stays current with my authentic desires

#### 3.1.3 Functional Requirements
- **FR-1.1**: Users can create and manage multiple life area categories
- **FR-1.2**: Users can add, edit, and delete dreams/goals within each life area
- **FR-1.3**: Users can view an integrated dashboard showing all life areas and their goals
- **FR-1.4**: Users can see connections and relationships between goals across different areas
- **FR-1.5**: Users can prioritize and organize goals within and across life areas
- **FR-1.6**: System provides visual representation of integrated life vision

#### 3.1.4 Non-Functional Requirements
- **NFR-1.1**: Data is stored securely and privately
- **NFR-1.2**: Interface is intuitive and easy to navigate
- **NFR-1.3**: Changes are saved automatically
- **NFR-1.4**: Users can export their vision data

### 3.2 Community Connection

#### 3.2.1 Feature Description
A social platform where users can connect with others, share experiences, ask questions, learn from peers, and build supportive relationships with people on similar journeys.

#### 3.2.2 User Stories
- As a user, I want to connect with others going through similar transitions so I feel less alone
- As a user, I want to share my experiences so others can learn from my journey
- As a user, I want to learn from others' experiences so I can navigate my transition better
- As a user, I want to ask questions and get support from the community

#### 3.2.3 Functional Requirements
- **FR-2.1**: Users can create profiles with relevant information about their journey
- **FR-2.2**: Users can discover and connect with other users
- **FR-2.3**: Users can share posts, updates, and reflections
- **FR-2.4**: Users can comment on and react to others' posts
- **FR-2.5**: Users can search and filter community content by topics, life areas, or transition types
- **FR-2.6**: Users can form or join groups based on shared interests or transition types
- **FR-2.7**: Users can send private messages to other users
- **FR-2.8**: System provides moderation tools to maintain supportive environment

#### 3.2.4 Non-Functional Requirements
- **NFR-2.1**: Community interactions are respectful and supportive
- **NFR-2.2**: Privacy controls allow users to manage their visibility
- **NFR-2.3**: Content is moderated to maintain safe space
- **NFR-2.4**: Real-time notifications for community interactions

### 3.3 Reflection Space

#### 3.3.1 Feature Description
A dedicated area for users to engage in reflection practices, journal about their journey, process experiences, and gain deeper insights into themselves and their path forward.

#### 3.3.2 User Stories
- As a user, I want a space to reflect on my journey so I can process my experiences
- As a user, I want to journal about my progress so I can track my growth
- As a user, I want reflection prompts to guide my thinking
- As a user, I want to look back at past reflections to see how I've grown

#### 3.3.3 Functional Requirements
- **FR-3.1**: Users can create and save reflection entries
- **FR-3.2**: Users can access guided reflection prompts
- **FR-3.3**: Users can organize reflections by date, topic, or life area
- **FR-3.4**: Users can search and filter past reflections
- **FR-3.5**: System can suggest reflection prompts based on user's goals and progress
- **FR-3.6**: Users can link reflections to specific goals or life areas
- **FR-3.7**: Users can set reflection reminders

#### 3.3.4 Non-Functional Requirements
- **NFR-3.1**: Reflections are completely private by default
- **NFR-3.2**: Data is encrypted and secure
- **NFR-3.3**: Interface supports rich text formatting
- **NFR-3.4**: Users can export their reflections

### 3.4 Accountability & Progress Tracking

#### 3.4.1 Feature Description
A comprehensive system that helps users stay accountable to their goals, track progress, visualize achievements, and maintain momentum toward building a meaningful life.

#### 3.4.2 User Stories
- As a user, I want to track my progress on my goals so I can see how far I've come
- As a user, I want to set milestones and deadlines so I stay accountable
- As a user, I want to celebrate my achievements so I maintain motivation
- As a user, I want to see visual representations of my progress
- As a user, I want reminders and check-ins to help me stay on track

#### 3.4.3 Functional Requirements
- **FR-4.1**: Users can link goals to progress tracking
- **FR-4.2**: Users can set milestones and deadlines for goals
- **FR-4.3**: Users can log progress updates and achievements
- **FR-4.4**: System provides visual progress indicators (charts, graphs, progress bars)
- **FR-4.5**: Users can set up accountability reminders and check-ins
- **FR-4.6**: Users can view progress history and trends over time
- **FR-4.7**: Users can celebrate milestones and achievements
- **FR-4.8**: System can suggest adjustments based on progress patterns
- **FR-4.9**: Users can share progress updates with accountability partners or community

#### 3.4.4 Non-Functional Requirements
- **NFR-4.1**: Progress data is accurate and reliable
- **NFR-4.2**: Visualizations are clear and meaningful
- **NFR-4.3**: Reminders are customizable and not overwhelming
- **NFR-4.4**: Progress tracking integrates with other features

## 4. Data Privacy & Transparency

### 4.1 Core Principles
- **User Ownership**: All insights and data belong to the user
- **Full Transparency**: Clear communication about what data is collected and how it's used
- **Purpose-Driven**: Data is only used to enhance user experience and provide personalized support
- **User Control**: Users have full control over their data and privacy settings

### 4.2 Data Collection
- User-provided content (goals, reflections, community posts)
- Usage patterns (feature engagement, progress tracking)
- Preferences and settings
- Community interactions (with user consent)

### 4.3 Data Usage
- Personalizing user experience
- Providing relevant insights and suggestions
- Improving platform features
- Connecting users with relevant community members (with consent)

### 4.4 Transparency Requirements
- **TR-1**: Clear privacy policy explaining all data collection
- **TR-2**: In-app indicators showing how data informs recommendations
- **TR-3**: User dashboard showing what data is stored
- **TR-4**: Easy-to-understand explanations of algorithms and suggestions
- **TR-5**: User control over data sharing and visibility

## 7. Success Criteria

### 7.1 MVP Launch Criteria
- All four core features are functional
- User authentication and data security are implemented
- Privacy and transparency features are in place
- Basic community moderation is active
- User onboarding flow is complete, including first vision creation
- Onboarding successfully guides users through creating their first vision

### 7.2 User Adoption Metrics
- Onboarding completion rate > 80%
- First vision creation completion rate > 75% (during or immediately after onboarding)
- 70% of users complete initial life vision setup (first vision created)
- 60% of users engage with at least 3 of 4 core features
- 50% of users return within first week
- 40% of users are active in community features
- 65% of users who complete onboarding create at least one additional vision within 30 days

### 7.3 User Satisfaction Metrics
- Net Promoter Score (NPS) > 50
- User satisfaction score > 4.0/5.0
- Feature-specific satisfaction scores
- Qualitative feedback on clarity and support

### 7.4 Vision Image Feature Stickiness & Quality Metrics

#### 7.4.1 Image Generation Success
- **Image Generation Completion Rate**: > 85% of users who start image generation complete it
- **Image Generation Time**: Average generation time < 60 seconds
- **Image Generation Retry Rate**: < 15% of users need to retry image generation
- **Skip Rate**: < 20% of users skip image generation during onboarding
- **First Image Satisfaction**: > 75% of users rate their first generated image as "good" or "excellent"

#### 7.4.2 Progressive Enhancement Adoption
- **Image Update Rate**: > 40% of users update their vision image at least once within 60 days of creation
- **Multiple Updates**: > 25% of users update their vision image 2+ times within 90 days
- **Update Frequency**: Average time between image updates < 30 days for active updaters
- **Suggestion Acceptance**: > 60% of users accept at least one system-suggested image update
- **Manual Update Rate**: > 50% of image updates are user-initiated (vs system-suggested)

#### 7.4.3 Feature Engagement
- **Vision Board Return Rate**: > 65% of users with vision images return to vision board within 7 days
- **Image Interaction Time**: Users spend average of 30+ seconds viewing/interacting with vision images per session
- **Progress Tracking Usage**: > 55% of users with trackable elements update at least one element within 30 days
- **Enhancement History Views**: > 35% of users view their image enhancement history at least once

#### 7.4.4 User Retention & Stickiness
- **7-Day Retention (with image)**: > 70% of users with vision images return within 7 days
- **7-Day Retention (without image)**: Comparison baseline (expected ~50%)
- **30-Day Retention (with updates)**: > 60% of users who update images return within 30 days
- **90-Day Active Users**: > 45% of users with vision images remain active at 90 days
- **Feature as Hook**: Vision image users show 1.5x higher retention vs non-image users

#### 7.4.5 Progress Correlation
- **Goal Completion Rate**: Users with vision images have > 1.3x goal completion rate vs without
- **Step Completion Rate**: > 50% correlation between image updates and increased step completions
- **Progress Velocity**: Users who update images show > 20% faster progress on their visions
- **Milestone Achievement**: > 40% of users update images after reaching milestones

#### 7.4.6 Quality Indicators
- **Image Relevance Score**: > 80% of images rated as "relevant to my vision" by users
- **Visual Appeal**: > 75% of users rate image aesthetic quality as "good" or "excellent"
- **Progress Tracking Clarity**: > 70% of users understand how to update images for progress
- **Feature Discovery**: > 60% of users discover the progressive enhancement feature within 30 days

#### 7.4.7 Technical Performance
- **Generation Success Rate**: > 95% of image generation attempts succeed without errors
- **API Response Time**: P95 < 45 seconds for image generation
- **Update Success Rate**: > 90% of image update attempts succeed
- **Error Recovery Rate**: > 85% of users who encounter errors retry and succeed

#### 7.4.8 Long-term Value
- **6-Month Active Users**: > 35% of users with vision images remain active at 6 months
- **Image Library Growth**: Average of 2+ vision images per active user at 6 months
- **Feature Satisfaction**: > 4.2/5.0 rating for vision image feature specifically
- **Word-of-Mouth**: > 40% of engaged users mention vision images when recommending the platform

#### 7.4.9 Key Success Indicators (KSIs)
The vision image feature is considered successful if:
1. **Adoption**: > 80% of onboarding users create a vision image
2. **Engagement**: > 50% of users with images update them at least once
3. **Retention**: Vision image users show > 1.4x retention at 30 days
4. **Satisfaction**: Feature scores > 4.0/5.0 in user feedback
5. **Impact**: Measurable positive correlation (> 1.2x) between image usage and goal achievement

#### 7.4.10 Measurement Approach
- **Analytics Events**: Track all image generation, updates, views, and interactions
- **User Surveys**: Quarterly surveys on feature satisfaction and usage
- **A/B Testing**: Compare user cohorts with and without vision image features
- **Qualitative Feedback**: Monthly review of user feedback and support tickets
- **Behavioral Analytics**: Monitor image update patterns, timing, and triggers
- **Cohort Analysis**: Track retention and engagement by image creation/update frequency
